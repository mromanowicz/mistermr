StoryDesk

On another project, we invented an easy way for people with little to no technical knowledge to build their own apps. We realized that the age of PowerPoint was over. Static presentations are going to the way of the dodo. An interactive well crafted narrative is the only way to tell compelling stories, and what better platform to do it on but the world's best consumption device—the iPad. So we did just that, we designed a set of tools so that whether you're an intern, or an account director, you could have your own app. We even cofounded a company around it called StoryDesk. Here's what people said: Wall Street Journal : "StoryDesk’s approach is to make what would be an otherwise dry PowerPoint deck a lot slicker and easier to navigate." Tech Crunch: "easy to use, even for those without technical expertise."
