EMAIL FOR THOSE WHO CAN SEE.

Visual Email is an email client built from the ground up for the iPad using a visual language and gestural controls.

It all started in Amsterdam

When we reinvented email for the iPad with Visual Email. It started with the thought that hey, we're sending a lot of images to our friends as a form of communication. We also noticed that a lot of marketers we're doing the same thing to us. Outside of this, we gathered that visual culture was on the rise with the popularity of Instagram and Pinterest.

So we thought, wouldn't it make sense if our inboxes were a series of images to represent what was really being sent to us, instead of the normal text based sender, subject line, timestamp that we're used to.
